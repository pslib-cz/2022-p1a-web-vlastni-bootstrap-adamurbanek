2023 Drivers

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is a Dutch racing driver who competes in Formula One with Red Bull Racing. He is the 2021 and 2022 Formula One World Champion, and the youngest and first Dutch driver to win a Formula One Grand Prix. He has 37 wins, 22 pole positions and two grand slams in his career. He is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen and karting champion Sophie Kumpen.

Perez has expressed his ambition to challenge Verstappen for the title and has asked for equal treatment from the team. Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso is a Spanish racing driver who competes for Aston Martin in Formula One. He is a two-time world champion, having won the title in 2005 and 2006 with Renault. He is also one of the most experienced and successful drivers in the history of the sport, with 358 Grand Prix starts and 33 race wins. He has driven for several top teams, including McLaren, Ferrari, and Alpine. He returned to Formula One in 2021 after a two-year hiatus, and has achieved three consecutive podiums at the start of the 2023 season.
